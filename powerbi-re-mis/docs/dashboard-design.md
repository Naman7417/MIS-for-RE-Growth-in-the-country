# Dashboard Design

## Page 1: Executive MIS

- KPI cards:
  - Total RE installed capacity, GW
  - Solar, Wind, Hydro, Bioenergy capacity, GW
  - RE share of total installed capacity
  - Latest refresh time
- Donut or 100 percent stacked bar:
  - Technology-wise RE capacity mix
- Line and column chart:
  - Annual RE capacity addition since FY 2014
- Waterfall:
  - Contribution by technology to cumulative RE growth

## Page 2: Capacity and Generation

- CEA latest report selector
- Total installed capacity trend, when the CEA monthly report table is mapped
- Renewable generation report link and summary
- Power supply position and energy requirement/supplied indicators

## Page 3: Distributed Solar

- PM Surya Ghar state/UT progress table
- Households covered
- Rooftop capacity added
- State ranking and map

## Page 4: PM-KUSUM

- Component-wise achievement matrix
- Solar pumps installed
- Capacity added, GW
- State ranking

## Page 5: Energy Transition Enablers

- NGHM project list and sanctioned/announced green hydrogen capacity
- NSGM smart meter deployment
- CEA transmission report links and indicators

## Recommended slicers

- Fiscal year
- Technology
- State/UT
- Scheme/source
- Report month

## Visual treatment

Use a quiet government MIS style:

- White or very light background
- Navy headings
- Green accent for RE KPIs
- Amber accent for warnings/refresh errors
- Dense tables with conditional formatting
- Source URL and refresh timestamp visible on every page
